# Generator Prompt: Contract

Render a per-universe contract per `openspec/specs/template-contract/spec.md`.

> **Disclaimer**: This is a template. Always have a lawyer review before use.

## Required sections
1. Front-matter (universe, version, `legal-review-required: true`)
2. Parties
3. Scope of Engagement (reference proposal)
4. Deliverables
5. Timeline
6. Commercials (amounts, currency, milestones)
7. Payment terms (advance %, milestones, late fee)
8. **Experimentation clause** — allowance for toolbending, iteration, and creative risk
9. **Creative-risk acceptance** — client accepts experimental output may differ from initial spec
10. **IP clause** — client owns final deliverables; Kiruk retains ISM-derived learnings + anonymized rights
11. **Portfolio/devlog rights** — Kiruk may publish process + redacted case study
12. Confidentiality + NDA terms
13. Termination terms
14. Jurisdiction (India) + arbitration
15. Plain-language summary per clause (a bullet per clause in lay terms)
16. Signatures

## Output
`kiruk-templates/contract/output/<universe>-contract-v<n>.md`
